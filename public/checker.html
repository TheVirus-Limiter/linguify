Script

A script is a type of writing with a specific alphabet which other alphabets are based upon. For example: Spanish is based upon the script language Latin

Autonym

A name used by a group or category of people to refer to themselves or their language

Direction

The direction a language is read and written. For English and most Indo-European languages, it is left to right.

Regions

The regions in which the language is officially spoken. For example, English is spoken almost everywhere. Available regions: Oceania, Asia, Africa, The Middle East, Europe, The Americas and Worldwide.
